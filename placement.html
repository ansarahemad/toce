Training & Placement Cell

<<<<<<< HEAD

Our College believes that the technical education should make the aspiring students find a job for their livelihood. The Placement and Training Cell of the college provides platform for the students to find a job of their choice. In order to make them fit enough to face the placement interview the college conducts many useful training programs. The Training and Placement cell is headed by a chief co-ordinator who is supported by seven members representing each department in addition to student representatives from all engineering branches.The institute has provided complete infrastructure for effective functioning of the cell. The available amenities include a spacious seminar hall, LCD, OHP and a conference hall for conducting interviews. TPC Cell takes initiative for developing right attitudes, soft skills, motivating our students to have strong technical as well as analytical capabilities, and exposing them to the requirements of corporate world and helping them in their professional careers.
